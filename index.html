Mission Statement:

From teamwork, to friendship, to technology, Jackson High School’s TSA Chapter embraces the TSA Motto of “Learning to Lead in a Technical World” to its fullest extent. We intend to prepare the doctors, engineers, mathematicians, and scientists of our future and to encourage all to strive for their dreams through learning, leadership, cooperation, and ambition.
